# Insight Agent

## Purpose

Convert analytical results and forecasts into human-readable explanations.

The Insight Agent uses Gemini to explain trends, anomalies, risks, and forecasts in natural language.

---

## Responsibilities

### Trend Explanation

Example:

AQI increased by 18% over the last month due to increased industrial activity and reduced rainfall.

---

### Forecast Interpretation

Example:

Traffic congestion is expected to increase during the next 14 days based on historical patterns and current growth trends.

---

### Risk Communication

Translate technical outputs into understandable explanations for decision-makers.

---

## Inputs

* Analytics results
* Forecast results
* KPI metrics

---

## Outputs

{
"insights": [],
"summary": "",
"risk_assessment": ""
}
